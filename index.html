What We Do

Oceans Are Rough

The maritime industry files $20B+ USD in insurance claims annually. The stakes are high, and keeping track of everything that happens on your vessel can be overwhelming.

We created Ocean Treasury to provide visual analytics for your fleets, so that your captain and crew can track the health of their vessel easily and securely. We know the oceans are rough, so when you need it, our software provides you the documentation you need for insurance claims so that you can recover the maximum value from your policy.

FAQ

WHY PARTNER WITH US?
We created Ocean Treasury as a result of our experiences working in the intersection of technology and supply chain. Our backgrounds span across maritime, insurance, supply chain, and deep technology. We've seen the impact technology can make at scale, and we are committed to improving maritime by building better software.
WHO IS OCEAN TREASURY FOR?
Ocean Treasury is purpose-built for ship owners and operators. We seek to work with teams who are tech-forward and want to be on the forefront of AI adoption in the maritime industry.
HOW IS MY DATA PROTECTED?
We process your CCTV data securely and indepedently from external databases. You retain control over all company and customer data and any confidential information. Ocean Treasury takes data security and privacy seriously. We store customer secrets such as API keys using secure methods, and are pursuing SOC2 certification.
WHAT AI DOES OCEAN TREASURY USE?
Ocean Treasury uses several different AI models to analyze and automate different steps of the insurance process, including large language models and image models. Some of these models are prompted and/or trained using our proprietary datasets which are specific to maritime operations.
