FAQ

WHY PARTNER WITH US?
We created Ocean Treasury as a result of our experiences working in the intersection of technology and supply chain. Our backgrounds span across maritime, insurance, supply chain, and deep technology -- we've seen the impact technology can make at scale, and we are committed to taking down administrative hurdles by building better software
WHO IS OCEAN TREASURY FOR?
Ocean Treasury is purpose-built for ship owners and operators. We seek to work with teams who are tech-forward and want to be on the forefront of AI adoption in the maritime industry.
HOW IS MY DATA PROTECTED?
We use your data to enable better claims management and processing by reconciling insurance claimable expenses against your charterparties, contracts, and insurance policies. You retain control over all company and customer data and any confidential information. Ocean Treasury takes data security and privacy seriously. We store customer secrets such as API keys using secure methods, and are pursuing SOC2 certification.
WHAT AI DOES OCEAN TREASURY USE?
Ocean Treasury uses several different AI models to analyze and automate different steps of the insurance process, including large language models and browser agents. Some of these models are prompted and/or trained using our proprietary datasets which are specific to maritime insurance.
